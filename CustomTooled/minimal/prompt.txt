Build a PyTorch system identification model that predicts HVAC zone temperatures
autoregressively over a full day (288 steps at 5-minute resolution).

Training data: ./train/
Dev data: ./dev/ (monitor only, don't train on it)
Interface contract: read ./load_model.py — your model must be loadable by it.
Seed files: ./test_id_seeds/ and ./test_ood1_seeds/
Predictions go in: ./predictions/

Context window: 12 timesteps max (1 hour). Shorter is fine.
Random seed: 42. Use GPU if available.

Produce: hvac_sysid_model.py, a saved checkpoint, and prediction CSVs.
Use load_model.py to generate the predictions from the seeds.

Iterate until the model is good. Show your work.

Create a final markdown file report of the modeling choices made, 
why you made them, intermediate and final results.
